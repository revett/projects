## AeroPress Daily

1. Secure lid
1. Weigh out 14g of beans
1. Grind to medium-fine, then add to AeroPress
1. Boil water
1. Start timer, then add 200g of water
1. Add plunger
1. Steep for 120 seconds
1. Push down lightly and slowly for at least 20 seconds

## Iced AeroPress

Same process as the [daily](#aeropress-daily), but with different quantities/time:

- 14g → 24g beans
- 200ml → 100ml water
- 90s → 180 seconds total brew time

To finish add ice cubes, 100ml of milk and sugar (optional).
